Questions? We Have Answers.

Is this legal? Can eToro detect automation? ▼

Yes. eToro explicitly supports API access and automation. They have an official public API. This is not against their terms. Many institutional traders use automation on eToro.

How much money do I need to start? ▼

Minimum: $500 to see any meaningful results. Recommended: $1,000+ for proper diversification across assets. The more capital, the more you benefit from compounding. Start small if you're nervous.

What's the win rate? Does it always work? ▼

No system wins 100%. My demo shows ~68% win rate historically. That means 68% of trades hit the profit target, 32% hit the stop loss. Over time, winners compound. But losses are real. Risk management is critical.

Can I customize the rules? ▼

Yes! Free Guide explains the rules. Advanced Guide includes code and customization examples. You can adjust profit targets, stop losses, asset allocation, cash ratios — anything. The system is yours to adapt.

How long is the setup? ▼

With Free Guide: 1-2 hours to understand the logic. With Advanced Guide: 30 minutes to implement + 1 hour to customize. eToro API connection is instant (OAuth2). First trades execute within a few minutes.

What if I lose money? ▼

Losses happen. The system has stop losses to limit downside, but markets can gap. Advanced Guide teaches risk management and position sizing. Start small. Test on paper trading first. Grow gradually as you gain confidence.
